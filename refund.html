Refund Policy

Last updated: 20 April 2026 · Operated by AdvoFin Consulting Pvt Ltd

1. Free trial period

All new Pro and Firm plan subscriptions include a 7-day free trial. You may cancel during the trial without any charge.

2. 7-day money-back guarantee

If you are not satisfied with NyayIQ within the first 7 days of your paid subscription, you may request a full refund. No questions asked.

3. After 7 days

After 7 days, subscription fees are non-refundable. However, you may cancel at any time and your access will continue until the end of your current billing cycle.

4. Yearly subscriptions

Yearly subscriptions are non-refundable after the 7-day window, except at our discretion. Upon cancellation, access continues until the end of the paid period.

5. Pro-rated upgrades and downgrades

Upgrades from Pro to Firm are pro-rated and take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing cycle.

6. Processing time

Approved refunds are processed via Razorpay within 5–7 business days and returned to the original payment method.

7. How to request a refund

Email contact@nyayiq.in with your account email, subscription details, and reason for refund. Our team responds within 24 hours.

8. Chargebacks

We encourage you to contact us before initiating a chargeback. Disputes are typically resolved quickly through direct communication.
