Cookie Policy

Last updated: 20 April 2026 · Operated by AdvoFin Consulting Pvt Ltd

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us recognize you, remember preferences, and understand usage.

2. Cookies we use

Essential cookies

These are necessary for NyayIQ to function — session management, authentication, and security. They cannot be disabled.

Preference cookies

These remember your settings — filter preferences, UI choices, and similar.

Analytics cookies

We use Plausible Analytics, a privacy-focused tool that does not track individual users and uses no identifying cookies.

3. Third-party cookies

Razorpay may set cookies during payment processing. Claude API requests are stateless and do not set cookies.

4. Your choices

You can control cookies via your browser settings — block, delete, or be notified of new cookies. Note that blocking essential cookies will prevent NyayIQ from functioning.

5. Do Not Track

We respect browser "Do Not Track" signals for analytics, though essential cookies remain necessary for service operation.

6. Updates

This policy may be updated. Material changes will be notified via email to registered users.
