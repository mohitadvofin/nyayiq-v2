Why NyayIQ exists.

Every morning, tax practitioners across India face the same problem — hundreds of new judgements, circulars, and notifications spread across multiple sources. Finding what's relevant takes hours. Understanding it takes more.

Over a decade of practicing tax litigation — drafting appeals, arguing before GST Appellate Authority, Delhi High Court, and ITAT — I saw how much time practitioners waste finding case law, let alone analyzing it.

The law doesn't lack good judgements. It lacks a good way to find and understand them quickly.

NyayIQ changes that. Every day, our automation collects the most important GST, Income Tax, Customs, and ITAT judgements. Each is analyzed by Claude AI — distilled into what actually matters: the legal issue, the ruling, and what it means for your clients.

On the AI behind NyayIQ.

NyayIQ uses Claude by Anthropic — Sonnet 4.5 and Opus 4.7 models. All AI summaries are generated from actual judgement text, grounded in verified citations.

AI can occasionally miss nuance. Every summary includes a confidence badge. Citations are verified against Indian Kanoon where possible. Always read the full judgement before relying on it.
