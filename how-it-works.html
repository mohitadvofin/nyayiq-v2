How It Works

From judgement to insight.

Every morning, automation does the research work. Every query, AI makes the synthesis. Your practice, amplified.

I.

Daily collection.

Before 7 AM IST every morning, our pipeline pulls fresh tax judgements from trusted sources — Indian Kanoon, Delhi HC website, ITAT portal, Supreme Court e-copy service.

  • Covers Supreme Court, all 25 High Courts, ITAT benches, GST Appellate Authority, CIT(A)
  • Focuses on GST, Income Tax, Customs, FEMA, and NCLT tax-related matters
  • De-duplicates and filters out routine administrative orders
  • Full text with original citations preserved
II.

AI analysis.

Each judgement is analyzed by Claude 4.5/4.7 — Anthropic's most accurate legal-reasoning model. We don't just summarize, we structure.

  • Extracts parties, court, bench, date of pronouncement
  • Identifies the legal issue and factual matrix
  • Separates ratio from obiter
  • Captures the holding and practical implication
  • Auto-tags with sections invoked (§74, §68, §148A, etc.)
III.

Smart categorization.

Every judgement gets tagged across multiple dimensions — so you find what you need in seconds.

  • By tax type: GST, Income Tax, Customs, FEMA
  • By court: SC, HC (state-wise), Tribunals
  • By outcome: Favour assessee, favour revenue, mixed
  • By impact: Landmark, binding, persuasive, distinguishable
  • By topic: Assessment, refund, penalty, ITC, TDS, etc.
IV.

Your dashboard.

The processed intelligence arrives in your inbox and dashboard. Search, filter, bookmark, export, or chat with it.

  • Morning email digest: top 5 must-read judgements
  • Semantic search: describe fact pattern, find similar cases
  • Ask follow-up questions via integrated chat
  • Upload your own PDF judgements for analysis
  • Export structured summaries to Word/PDF for briefs
