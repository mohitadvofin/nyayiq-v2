Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 April 2026 · Operated by AdvoFin Consulting Pvt Ltd

1. Introduction

AdvoFin Consulting Pvt Ltd ("we", "us", "NyayIQ") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use NyayIQ at nyayiq.in.

This policy is framed in compliance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act) and applicable Indian laws.

2. Information we collect

Account information

When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, password (hashed), designation, and firm name.

Usage information

We track which judgements you view, search queries, AI interactions, and bookmarks to provide the service.

Payment information

Payment processing is handled by Razorpay. We do not store card details.

3. How we use your information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve NyayIQ
  • To process your subscription and send transactional emails
  • To send the daily tax digest if you have opted in
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Data retention & location

All data is stored in Mumbai, India (Supabase ap-south-1 region). Data is retained for the duration of your active account. Upon account deletion, data is purged within 30 days, subject to legal retention requirements.

5. Your rights under DPDP Act

You have the right to access, correct, and delete your personal data. You may also withdraw consent and request data portability. To exercise these rights, email contact@nyayiq.in.

6. Third-party services

We share limited data with Claude API (Anthropic) for AI processing, Razorpay for payments, Brevo for emails, and Plausible for privacy-respecting analytics. None of these parties sell your data.
