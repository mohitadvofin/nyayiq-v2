Frequently asked questions.

What counts as an AI Summary?
Each time you click "AI Summary" and get a structured analysis. Cached summaries don't count.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes — no long-term commitment. Access continues until end of current billing period.
Is my payment secure?
Processed through Razorpay, PCI DSS compliant. We never store card details.
Do you provide GST invoice?
Yes, GST-compliant invoices issued by AdvoFin Consulting Pvt Ltd. Add your GSTIN for B2B.
What is your refund policy?
Full refund if not satisfied within 7 days. After 7 days, subscriptions are non-refundable but cancellable.
Can I change plans later?
Yes. Upgrade anytime with pro-rated billing. Downgrades at end of cycle.
Are AI summaries reliable for court filings?
AI is research assistance, not substitute. Always verify the original text before citing.
